**所有 prompt 共享的 CS 视觉语法：**
Cultist Simulator card art uses single-hue washes on square canvases, sparse woodcut-style linework, no gradients, no crosshatching, no second color. Every sprite looks like a zinc-plate engraving from an 1890s occult press — not illustrated, but printed. The game's card sprites are 256×256, filled edge-to-edge with a translucent principle-tinted wash, with the subject drawn in a deeper tone of the same hue.
